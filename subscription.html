Auto-Renewal Service Rules

Effective date: May 27, 2026

These Rules are supplementary rules for Breathe Well Pro auto-renewable subscriptions. If you use the auto-renewal service, you are deemed to have agreed to these Rules.

You understand and agree that:

1. Service name: Breathe Well Pro auto-renewal service.

2. This service is provided for users who purchase a Breathe Well Pro auto-renewable subscription through Apple in-app purchase.

3. Available subscription plans, prices, free trials, discounts, billing periods, and benefits are subject to the information shown on the in-app purchase page and Apple's purchase confirmation page.

4. After you confirm purchase and enable auto-renewal, Apple will charge the payment method associated with your Apple ID within 24 hours before the current subscription period expires, unless auto-renewal is cancelled at least 24 hours before the expiration time.

5. If the payment method balance is insufficient, expired, unavailable, or otherwise fails, renewal may fail. The consequences of failed renewal are borne by you.

6. After successful renewal, the Breathe Well Pro validity period will be extended by the corresponding subscription period.

7. If you purchase different Pro subscription plans at different times, the final subscription status, billing rules, and validity period are subject to Apple's subscription management and confirmation results.

Cancellation Method

To cancel auto-renewal, please do so at least 24 hours before the current subscription expires.

Cancellation path: open iPhone Settings, tap your Apple ID, enter Subscriptions, select Breathe Well, and cancel the subscription.

You may also manage subscriptions through App Store account settings. The exact path may differ depending on iOS version and region.

Refunds

Subscription payments are processed by Apple. Refund requests and refund results are subject to Apple's rules and review.

生效日期：2026 年 5 月 27 日

本规则为好呼吸 Pro 自动续费订阅的补充规则。如您使用自动续费服务，即视为您同意本规则。

您理解并同意：

1、服务名称：好呼吸 Pro 自动续费服务。

2、本服务是在您通过 Apple 应用内购买开通好呼吸 Pro 自动续费订阅的前提下，为您提供的自动续费服务。

3、可选订阅方案、价格、免费试用、优惠、计费周期及会员权益，以应用内购买页面及 Apple 购买确认页面展示的信息为准。

4、您确认购买并开通自动续费后，除非您至少在当前订阅期到期前 24 小时取消自动续费，Apple 将在当前订阅期到期前 24 小时内，从您的 Apple ID 绑定付款方式中扣取下一周期费用。

5、如付款方式余额不足、过期、不可用或其他原因导致扣款失败，则可能导致续费失败，由此产生的后果由您自行承担。

取消方式

如需取消自动续费，请至少在当前订阅期到期前 24 小时操作取消。

取消路径：打开 iPhone“设置”，点击您的 Apple ID，进入“订阅”，选择“好呼吸”，取消订阅。

您也可以通过 App Store 账户设置管理订阅。具体路径可能因 iOS 版本和地区而有所不同。

退款

订阅付款由 Apple 处理。退款申请及退款结果，适用 Apple 的相关规则和审核结果。
