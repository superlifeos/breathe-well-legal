Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026

This Policy applies to Breathe Well, including the iOS app, Lock Screen activities, widgets, and related support services.

By using the app, you understand and agree to the information handling described below.

1. Scope

(A) This Policy applies to information generated when you use breathing sessions, reminders, personalization settings, Apple Health writing, iCloud sync, Pro purchases, and support contact.

(B) This Policy does not apply to services independently provided by Apple or other third parties. Their own rules apply when you use those services.

2. Information Collection

(A) The app may store your breathing mode, duration, cycle count, custom breathing rhythm, reminder time, language, theme, visual style, sound, voice cue, haptic preference, favorite mode, hidden mode, and Pro status on your device.

(B) If you authorize Apple Health, the app writes completed breathing sessions to Apple Health as mindful minutes. The app does not read your Apple Health data.

(C) If you enable reminders, the app saves reminder times and schedules local notifications on your device.

(D) If you purchase Breathe Well Pro, the purchase and subscription status is processed by Apple through App Store and StoreKit. The app does not receive your bank card number or complete payment information.

(E) If iCloud sync is available and enabled for your Pro features, settings and custom modes may be synced between your devices through Apple's iCloud key-value storage.

(F) If you contact support by email, the email address and content you provide will be used to handle the request.

3. Information Use

(A) The above information is used to provide breathing guidance, save preferences, schedule reminders, restore purchases, sync settings, write mindful minutes with authorization, and respond to support requests.

(B) The app does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

(C) The app does not use third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

4. Information Disclosure

The app may disclose or process relevant information in the following situations:

(A) With your prior consent;

(B) When necessary to provide Apple platform services such as App Store, StoreKit, iCloud, Apple Health, notifications, and Live Activities;

(C) When required by applicable laws, regulations, administrative authorities, or judicial authorities;

(D) When necessary to protect user safety, app security, or legal rights.

5. Information Storage

(A) Local settings are stored on your device until you change them, reset device data, or delete the app.

(B) iCloud sync data is stored and managed by Apple under your Apple ID settings.

(C) Apple Health records are stored in Apple Health and can be managed in the Health app.

(D) Support emails are retained only as needed to process requests and keep necessary records.

6. Information Security

The app uses platform security measures such as system permissions, iOS sandboxing, and Apple services to protect information. Please understand that no network transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Your Choices

(A) You may manage Apple Health permissions in system settings or delete related records in the Health app.

(B) You may disable reminders in the app or disable notification permission in system settings.

(C) You may manage iCloud sync through Apple ID or iCloud settings.

(D) You may manage subscriptions, cancellations, and refunds through Apple ID or App Store settings.

(E) You may delete the app to remove local app data from your device.

8. Children

The app does not provide services specifically directed to children and does not knowingly collect children's personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact support.

9. Changes

This Policy may be updated when the app changes or when legal requirements change. The updated Policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Policy, please contact: hi.superlifeos@gmail.com.

本政策适用于好呼吸，包括 iOS 应用、锁屏实时活动、小组件及相关支持服务。

您使用本应用，即表示您了解并同意本政策所述的信息处理方式。

1. 适用范围

(A) 本政策适用于您使用呼吸练习、提醒、个性化设置、Apple 健康写入、iCloud 同步、Pro 购买及支持联系时产生的信息。

(B) 本政策不适用于 Apple 或其他第三方独立提供的服务。您使用该等服务时，应同时遵守其相应规则。

(B) 如您授权 Apple 健康，本应用会将完成的呼吸练习写入 Apple 健康的“正念分钟”。本应用不会读取您的 Apple 健康数据。

(D) 如您购买好呼吸 Pro，购买与订阅状态由 Apple 通过 App Store 和 StoreKit 处理。本应用不会获得您的银行卡号或完整付款信息。

(F) 如您通过邮件联系支持，您提供的邮箱地址及邮件内容将用于处理该请求。

3. 信息使用

(A) 上述信息用于提供呼吸引导、保存偏好、安排提醒、恢复购买、同步设置、在授权后写入正念分钟，以及回复支持请求。

(C) 本应用不使用第三方广告 SDK 或第三方分析 SDK。

在以下情况下，本应用可能披露或处理相关信息：

(A) 经您事先同意；

(B) 为提供 App Store、StoreKit、iCloud、Apple 健康、通知、实时活动等 Apple 平台能力所必需；

(C) 根据适用法律、法规、行政机关或司法机关要求；

(D) 为保护用户安全、应用安全或合法权益所必需。

(A) 本地设置会保存在您的设备上，直到您更改设置、重置设备数据或删除应用。

(B) iCloud 同步数据由 Apple 依据您的 Apple ID 设置进行存储和管理。

(D) 支持邮件仅在处理请求和保留必要记录所需期间保存。

(B) 您可以在应用内关闭提醒，或在系统设置中关闭通知权限。

(E) 您可以删除本应用，以移除设备上的本地应用数据。
